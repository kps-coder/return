Inclusive Funds Support

Smile Microcredit will fund small scale businesses with startup funds, operation funds, living funds with a low interest rate to help solve financial problems.

  • Startup Maintenance Funds Up to 70 million Won (Yearly 4.5% Interest Rate)
    To small scale businesses, people who wish to establish
  • Living Funds Up to 12 million Won (Yearly 3% Interest Rate)
    To the vulnerable social group like the disabled, single parented families, multi-cultured families, North Korean escapees etc.
  • Maintenance Funds Up to 10 million Won (Yearly 4.5% Interest Rate)
    To traditional market small business owners
