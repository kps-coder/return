Guaranteed Loan Support

Support to get loans at a low interest rates at formal financial institutions like banks.

  • Sunshine Loan Standard Up to 15 Million Won (Yearly within 12.5% Interest Rate)
    Loan Support
    To low-income individuals with low credit score
  • Sunshine Loan Special Up to 10 Million Won (Yearly within 12.5% Interest Rate)
    Loan Support
    To low-income individuals with lowest credit score, particularly those for whom using high-interest loans form lenders or illegal private finance is unavoidable
  • Sunshine Loan Youth Up to 12 Million Won (Yearly 3.6~1.5% Interest Rate)
    Studies, Employment preparation Funds
    To college students, young adults
  • Sunshine Loan – Credit Card Up to 3 Million Won (Yearly 3.6~1.5% Interest Rate)
    Allowing low credit people to issue credit cards
  • Sunshine Loan – 119 Up to 20 Million Won (Yearly within 7% Interest Rate)
    Operating funds
    To Sole proprietors enrolled in bank debt restructuring programs
  • Regional Government-Backed Guarantee Programs (Limit and rates may vary by product)
    Loan Support
    A collaborative financial program between KINFA and local governments, designed to support residents in the region
