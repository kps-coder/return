Non-Financial Servicest

Help underprivileged people in the lower income bracket become independent and regain their feet

All-round consultation
A counseling service designed to offer financial diagnosis, tailored loan, search the service for dormant deposit to reclaim money, and a consulting service for welfare all in one place through 50 nationwide financial inclusive centers

• How to apply: 1397 Inclusive Finance Call Center

Credit and debt management consulting service
1:1 tailored credit and debt management coaching of financial experts (up to 6 months)

• How to apply: App or website of Korea Inclusive Finance Agency / 1397 Inclusive Finance Call Center

Consulting service for sole proprietors
Tailored management solutions for sole proprietors who use political microcredit products

• How to apply: Website for a consulting service for sole proprietors (https://con.kinfa.or.kr↗)

Employment support
Information on the employment support service for non-employed people in the lower income bracket and find jobs for them

• How to apply: Employment Support Portal (https://job.kinfa.or.kr↗)

Financial education
Tailored online and offline financial education for people in the lower income bracket and young adults in need of financial education

• How to apply: Financial Education Portal (https://edu.kinfa.or.kr↗)
