Message from the CEO

Greetings, and welcome to the
Korea Inclusive Finance Agency (KINFA) website.

I am Eun-kyung Kim, President of KINFA.

KINFA is dedicated to providing policy-based inclusive financial support,
ensuring that every citizen has equal access to a robust financial safety net without discrimination.

Policy-based inclusive finance serves as an institutional safeguard, securing the fundamental right to financial access for those who are vulnerable to market marginalization. As the core financial infrastructure of the Republic of Korea, KINFA is committed to ensuring that this right translates into a tangible reality.

We are continually expanding access to institutional finance through various loan guarantee programs, including the Sunshine Loan (Haetsalron) general and special guarantees, as well as the Sunshine Loan Youth. Furthermore, by offering low-interest direct loan products, we strive to protect the financially vulnerable who face high risks from illegal private lending.

In addition, we actively support young adults—our future generation—in establishing sound financial habits and building stable wealth through dedicated savings products, financial counseling, and educational programs.

Coupled with these efforts, we provide customized, comprehensive assistance that integrates employment and welfare services. By offering self-sufficiency programs such as credit and debt management counseling, self-employment consulting, and financial education, we are building a holistic support framework that enables individuals to grow alongside finance.

Moving forward, KINFA will dedicate its utmost efforts to ensuring that finance is recognized and guaranteed as a "fundamental right," empowering the working class and vulnerable populations to achieve true economic independence.

We humbly ask for your continued interest, invaluable advice, and warm encouragement.

Thank you.

Eun-kyung Kim President of KINFA
