Message from the CEO

Greetings, and welcome to the
Korea Inclusive Finance Agency (KINFA) website.

I am Eun-kyung Kim, President of KINFA.

KINFA is dedicated to providing policy-based inclusive financial support,
ensuring that every citizen has equal access to a robust financial safety net without discrimination.

In accordance with the “Microfinance Support Act”, we continue to offer more opportunities for people in the lower income bracket with a lower credit score to easily access financial services and help them become independent and get back on their feet through enhanced financial capabilities.

By offering a comprehensive range of financial services –from the Sunshine Loan series (including Standard, Youth, Card) and Asset Accumulation Support to Smile Microloans – and non-financial support such as credit consulting and employment assistance, we are ushering in a new era of inclusive finance

In addition, we manage dormant deposit that have long been inactive to grant easier and quicker access for original account holders to reclaim their funds.

We will remain committed to providing a wide variety of products and services to create a better life for economically vulnerable people.

We are deeply grateful for your continued affection and warm support.

Thank you.

Eun-kyung Kim President of KINFA
