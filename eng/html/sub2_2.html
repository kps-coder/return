Inclusive Loan Support

Provide low-interest startup, operating, and living funds through direct loans and microcredits to empower small businesses and individuals.

  • Illegal Private Finance Prevention Loan Up to 1 Million Won (Yearly within 12.5% Interest Rate)
    Loan Support
    To those who excluded from all institutional finance, including money lenders

    ※ Interest rate for Socially Vulnerable Groups : 9.9% per annum
    ※ Interest rate for Repeat Borrowers : 4.5% per annum
  • Smile Microcredit Living Funds for the Financially Vulnerable Up to 50 Million Won (Yearly within 4.5% Interest Rate)
    Living Loans
    To young adults aged (19-34) unemployed or within their first year of employment/startup, loyal Smile Microcredit users and other financially vulnerable groups

    ※ supported through Smile Microcredit delivery partners
  • Smile Microcredit Startup and Operating Funds Up to 70 Million Won (rates may vary by product)
    Startup and Business Operating Loans
    To low-income or low credit, recipients of basic livelihood security, or the near-poverty group, among others

    ※ supported through Smile Microcredit delivery partners, private service agencies, and traditional market merchant associations
