Personalized Loan Service

KINFA informs clients to personalized Inclusive Finance Product Policies and loan product

  1. 1. Enter loan evaluation information
  2. 2. Offer/request available loan products
  3. 3. Provide available loan products
  4. 4. Guide available loan products
  5. 5. Choose product after comparison
  6. 6. Request evaluation of chosen product
  7. 7. After evaluation, loan

Personalized Loan Services (loan.kinfa.or.kr) is a loan information synthesis platform where users can easily and quickly, compare and apply at once, to various financial companies’ loan products such as banks or saving banks, by entering needed information about loans.

Clients can compare different companies loan products at once and choose the most optimum custom loan product.
