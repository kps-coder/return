Protection of Original Right Holders

Dormant Deposit protects the original right holder.

The original right holders who contributed their dormant deposit to KINFA, can visit a Financial Inclusive Center or go online (sleepmoney.kinfa.or.kr) and verify and receive payment.

Dormant Deposit: a deposit, saving or installment etc. which was not claimed after a time
