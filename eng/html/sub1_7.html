Intoduction

Korea Inclusive Finance Agency is a quasi-governmental institution designed to provide stability in the financial life of people in the lower income bracket and foster a balanced economic and social development under the “Microfinance Support Act.”

We have integrated previously scattered services throughout the Smile Loan Central Foundation, National Happiness Fund, and Korea Mortgage Loan to allow people in the lower income bracket and vulnerable classes to easily access microfinance services all in one place.

Our financial services such as fund support and the guarantee service as well as non-financial services, including all-round consulting service, credit/debt management consulting, consulting for sole proprietors, employment support, and financial education allow us to display ceaseless growth as an agency designed to provide inclusive finance to help people in the lower income bracket get back on their feet.
